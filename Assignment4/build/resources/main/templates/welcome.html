Welcome to The Hangman Game!

Welcome to my online html-based Hangman game using Spring Boot and Thymeleaf.

Author: Brandon Ha 301333647 bbha@sfu.ca

Rules: Players guess a letter and continues to guess until they have guessed the word or reached 8 incorrect guesses.

Players can guess the same letter and if the letter is part of the word, it will be counted as a guess but not an incorrect guess.
